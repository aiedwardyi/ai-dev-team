# ═══════════════════════════════════════
# ARCHETYPE 25: BLOG / PERSONAL
# ═══════════════════════════════════════

PATH: A (Tailwind + Alpine)
Use for: personal blogs, newsletters, thought leadership, writer portfolios, indie publications.
Theme: Elegant, content-focused, and highly readable.
Fonts:
  - Headings: `Instrument Serif` (display serif, `font-weight: 700` for H1, `600` for H2/H3). Use `font-feature-settings: "liga", "clig", "kern";` for enhanced legibility.
  - Body: `Inter` (sans-serif, `font-weight: 400` for prose, `500` for metadata).
Accent:
  - Light Theme: Primary accent `#0F766E` (deep teal), Secondary accent `#D97706` (amber-600).
  - Dark Theme: Primary accent `#34D399` (emerald-400), Secondary accent `#FBBF24` (amber-400).
Rules:
  - **Content-first:** Prioritize readability and clear presentation of text and images.
  - **Typography Precision:**
    - Main hero title (`H1`): `text-5xl` to `text-6xl` on desktop, `font-weight: 700`, `line-height: 1.1`.
    - Section titles (`H2`): `text-3xl` to `text-4xl`, `font-weight: 600`, `line-height: 1.25`.
    - Body prose: `text-lg`, `line-height: 1.75`, `max-w-prose` (`max-w-2xl` equivalent) for optimal reading width.
    - Metadata (author, date, read time): `text-sm`, `font-weight: 500`, `text-gray-600` (light) / `text-gray-400` (dark).
    - "FEATURED ARTICLE" tag: `text-xs` (uppercase, `letter-spacing: 0.05em`), `font-weight: 600`, in accent color.
  - **Color System:**
    - Light Theme: `bg-[#FDFBF9]`, `text-[#1A1A1A]`, secondary text `text-[#6B7280]`, subtle borders `border-[#E5E7EB]`.
    - Dark Theme: `bg-[#1A1A1A]`, `text-[#FDFBF9]`, secondary text `text-[#9CA3AF]`, subtle borders `border-[#374151]`.
    - Buttons and interactive elements use the primary accent color. Category tags use secondary accent color.
  - **Depth & Polish:**
    - Article cards and newsletter signup block MUST have `border-radius: 0.75rem` (`rounded-xl`).
    - Use subtle `shadow-md` on cards and interactive elements.
    - Hero section should have a subtle background image with a `linear-gradient` overlay for text contrast, or a `backdrop-filter: blur(8px)` on the text container if a full image is behind it.
  - **Layout Precision:**
    - Main content wrapper `max-w-screen-xl` (`1280px`) with `px-4` or `px-8` horizontal padding.
    - Sections separated by `py-20` or `py-24`.
    - Article grid `grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-2 lg:grid-cols-3 gap-8`.
    - Author bio text block should have a `max-w-3xl` width, centered, to avoid awkward narrowness.
  - **Interactivity Cues:**
    - All buttons and links MUST have distinct `hover:bg-opacity-80` or `hover:shadow-lg hover:translate-y-[-2px]` effects with `transition-all duration-200 ease-in-out`.
    - Article cards MUST have a subtle `hover:shadow-xl hover:scale-[1.01]` effect.
    - Dark/light toggle MUST be present and functional, with a smooth transition.
  - **Anti-patterns to eliminate:**
    - NEVER use generic purple/blue gradients for accents.
    - NEVER use "Article Thumbnail" as image placeholders. All images MUST be unique and relevant.
    - NO hard-to-read text due to insufficient contrast.
    - AVOID flat, lifeless layouts. Add subtle depth.
MUST include:
  - **Minimal Navigation:** Top-right, with `Home`, `Categories`, `About` links, and a functional dark/light mode toggle (sun/moon icon).
  - **Featured Post Hero Section:** Prominent, full-width or near full-width, with a large title, concise description, author, date, read time, category tag, and a clear "Read More" button. Background image relevant to the article.
  - **Recent Posts Grid (6-9 posts):** A visually appealing grid of article cards. Each card MUST include:
    - High-quality, relevant image (aspect ratio 16:9 or 4:3, `rounded-lg`).
    - Category tag (using secondary accent color).
    - Descriptive title (min 5 words).
    - Short excerpt (2-3 lines).
    - Author, date, read time.
    - "Read More" link.
  - **Explore Topics Section:** A horizontal scrollable list of category pills/buttons.
  - **Newsletter Signup Block:** A visually distinct section with a compelling headline, brief description, email input field (`placeholder="Enter your email"`), and a primary accent-colored "Subscribe" button. Include a relevant, high-quality image or illustration.
  - **Author Bio Section:** Centered, with a professional circular avatar image (NOT initials), author's name, and a 3-4 sentence descriptive bio.
  - **Minimal Footer:** Copyright, Privacy Policy, Terms of Service links.
